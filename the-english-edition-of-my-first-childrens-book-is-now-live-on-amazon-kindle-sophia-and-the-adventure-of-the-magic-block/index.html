The English edition of my first children's book is now live on Amazon Kindle: Sophia and the Adventure of the Magic Block!

June 13, 2025 in writing, publishing, bitcoin

Most kids' books don’t teach anything about money, let alone decentralized systems like Bitcoin. Mine does and it is finally available in English on Amazon Kindle: Sophia and the Adventure of the Magic Block.

This is a plain text story, no illustrations yet, but it is written to spark curiosity in both children and adults, especially around the ideas of cooperation, innovation and how trust can be built without central authority. You can now read the entire book directly below.

Sophia and the Adventure of the Magic Block

Welcome to Bitville

One sunny morning, the city of Bitville sparkled brighter than ever. The colorful houses reflected the sunlight, and the cobblestone streets shimmered with life. Everything in Bitville ran on special coins called Bitcoin Coins. These coins brought magic and energy to every corner of the city, helping everyone do things more easily and make them more beautiful.

Sophia, a curious 6-year-old girl, was excitedly getting ready for her first day of school. On her way, she met new friends:

  • Oliver, who loved building and solving puzzles
  • Ella, always ready to help anyone
  • Quincy, an expert in secret codes and special messages
  • Fiona, full of energy and creativity
  • Liam, the town’s storyteller who brought smiles with his tales

Sophia’s First Day of School

At school, the teacher told the children how to use and protect Bitcoin Coins. Sophia was fascinated by all the new things she was learning, listening carefully to every word.

After class, the kids gathered in the schoolyard. Liam started telling them a story about the Great Halving, an event that happened every four years.

“That’s when Bitcoin Coins become harder to find,” he explained.
“But it encourages us to be more creative and find new ways to discover them.”

The Great Bitville Challenge

Sophia looked at her friends, thinking of a solution.
“Hey! I know how we can help Bitville!” she shouted excitedly.

Oliver grinned. “We could build a new device!”
Quincy nodded. “Or create a special code that helps us find more coins.”
Ella smiled too. “We all have great ideas. We just need to work together!”

Sophia and Her Bright Idea

That evening, at home, Sophia thought about everything she had learned.

“If we found the Magic Block, we could help everyone advance with technology and keep Bitville shining and full of magic!”

The next day, she shared her idea with her friends:

“Let’s go on an adventure to find the Magic Block!”

Preparing for the Adventure

Full of excitement, the kids got ready:

  • Oliver packed his toolbox.
  • Quincy brought his notebook of codes and puzzles.
  • Fiona packed tasty snacks and fun games.
  • Liam prepared inspiring stories.
  • Ella brought an old map of Bitville.
  • Sophia came ready with her new knowledge.

Meeting Victor

While they were preparing, Victor, a skilled craftsman, saw them.

“What are you kids up to?” he asked.
“We’re going to find the Magic Block!” said Fiona.

Victor sighed. “Why not use my tools? They’re good.”
Sophia smiled. “Mr. Victor, if you join us, we can discover new things together and help Bitville shine even brighter!”
After a moment, Victor agreed.

“All right, I’ll come with you.”

The Cunning Shadow Zero

On their journey, the kids felt a strange presence. A dark shadow named Zero tried to stand in their way. He wanted to stop Bitville’s progress and keep the magic all for himself.

Facing the Challenges

Zero set trap after trap: disappearing bridges, shifting paths, and tricky riddles. But the kids worked together:

  • Quincy cracked the codes
  • Oliver fixed the bridges
  • Liam lifted spirits with stories
  • Fiona entertained everyone
  • Ella guided them with the map
  • Sophia solved problems using what she had learned in school
  • Victor contributed his tools and wisdom

Discovering the Magic Block

After many adventures, they reached a hidden clearing. In the center was an old chest, locked with a complicated code.

“I think this is the Magic Block!” Sophia exclaimed.

Quincy leaned in and quickly solved the puzzle.
The lock opened, revealing the Magic Block, glowing like a star. Its light surrounded them, filling them with energy and joy. They knew this discovery would help Bitville shine brighter than ever.

Defeating Zero

The heroes returned to Bitville and placed the Magic Block in the Central Plaza. It began to shine brightly, and the light spread in all directions. The shadow of Zero vanished completely.

With Zero defeated, the townspeople used the Magic Block to invent new tools and find more Bitcoin Coins. The city glowed even more beautifully than before.

The Celebration in Bitville

Everyone celebrated the children’s success. Lights danced in the sky. Music played. Laughter echoed through the town.

Victor shared his tools freely, realizing the power of sharing and embracing new ideas.

Lessons Learned

Through their adventure, Sophia and her friends discovered:

  • Education and innovation help everyone
  • Teamwork can overcome any obstacle
  • Welcoming new ideas brings happiness and progress

A New Adventure Awaits

At the end of the celebration, Sophia found a mysterious book in her backpack, full of inventions and maps of unknown places.

Looking at her friends, she smiled and said:

“Our adventure is just beginning!”

Everyone smiled, ready for the next chapter.
